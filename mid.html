About us

La Verte is a cutie tea room serving cutie meals with a view to bringing to the public a green, relaxing space.

No visit to Adam Town is complete without a trip to our awesome Tea Room. With beautiful interiors inspired by the Queen Mary ocean liner, it’s been making a splash since 1936.

In addition to our full green latte and crepe bar menu, we serve fresh made-to-order breakfast and lunch sandwiches, as well as a selection of sides and salads and other good stuff.
